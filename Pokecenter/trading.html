In select regions*, our Pokemon Centers have an area especially designed to help you connect with your friends! Trade, battle, or just hang out to your heart's content!

For connections with friends nearby, use the Cable Club. For connections with friends far away, use the Wireless Club. For connections with friendly strangers, use the Global Trade System.

*Available in Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, and Unova locations.
