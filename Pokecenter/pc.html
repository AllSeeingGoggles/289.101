PC

Pokemon team organisation is no longer a hassle! The PC is a computer next to the main counter that lets you tranfer Pokemon to and from your team, as well as organise your boxed Pokemon (the Pokemon not on your team). It's completely free, and easy to use! It can even store items!

How to swap Pokemon in your party:

Step 1: Access the PC (located next to the main counter). Ask one of our friendly staff members if you can't find it!

(In Galar, the PC may be inhabited by a Rotom. They're safe, and happy to help!)

Step 2: Select "Move Pokemon".

"Withdraw Pokemon" and "Deposit Pokemon" are options, but they're designed for people experienced with the PC already.

Step 3: Select the Pokemon you want to move into or out of your party.

(Select "Party Pokemon" to see the Pokemon currently in your party, if you want to put them in the PC.)

Step 4: Select "Move".

Step 5: Move the Pokemon to the spot you want them to be.

Step 6: Select "Place". If you chose a spot currently occupied by another Pokemon, this process will swap their places!

Your Pokemon should now be wherever you placed them. When you're ready to leave, select "Close Box".
